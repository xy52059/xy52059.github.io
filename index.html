Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

BigPic ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, such as device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID) through our third-party partners to provide analytics and advertising services.
  • Image Data: Our AI upscaling is processed locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or share your personal images to any server. Your photos remain entirely under your control.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app (e.g., features used, session duration) is collected via third-party SDKs.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party SDKs to provide essential services. These providers may collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used for providing personalized and non-personalized advertisements.
    View AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Umeng SDK: Used for app analytics, performance monitoring, and advertising optimization.
    View Umeng Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare: Used for region detection to provide localized services and optimize network performance.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the information we collect. Since your images are processed locally, the risk of data exposure is significantly minimized. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or California (USA), you have specific rights regarding your data:

  • The right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you.
  • The right to opt-out of personalized advertising.
  • You can manage these choices at any time through the "Privacy Settings" menu within the BigPic app.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
